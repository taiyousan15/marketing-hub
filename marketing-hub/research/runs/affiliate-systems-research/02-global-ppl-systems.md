## 1. Overview of PPL Affiliate Marketing

### Definition
Pay-Per-Lead (PPL), also known as Cost-Per-Lead (CPL), is an affiliate marketing model where advertisers pay affiliates for each qualified lead generated, regardless of whether the lead converts to a sale.

### Lead Types
- Email newsletter signups
- Free trial registrations
- Quote requests
- Form submissions
- App downloads (CPI)
- Account creations

### Advantages
| For Advertisers | For Affiliates |
|-----------------|----------------|
| Lower risk (pay only for leads) | Easier conversion (no purchase required) |
| Predictable cost per acquisition | Faster earnings |
| Build sales pipeline | Works for all niches |
| Qualify leads before sales | Lower traffic requirements |

## 6. Commission Models Deep Dive

### Pay-Per-Lead (CPL)
```
Payment trigger: Form submission, signup, registration
Typical rates: $1-100 depending on vertical
Validation: May require confirmation
```

### Pay-Per-Sale (CPS)
```
Payment trigger: Completed purchase
Typical rates: 5-50% of sale value
Validation: After refund window
```

### Pay-Per-Click (CPC)
```
Payment trigger: Click on affiliate link
Typical rates: $0.01-2 per click
Validation: Fraud detection required
```

### Revenue Share
```
Payment trigger: Ongoing customer spending
Typical rates: 10-50% of revenue
Duration: Lifetime or fixed period
```

### Hybrid Models
```
Combination: CPL + CPS
Example: $5 per lead + 10% of sales
Incentivizes: Quality lead generation
```

## 7. Global Trends (2024-2026)

### Cookie Deprecation Impact
- Third-party cookies phasing out
- S2S tracking becoming standard
- First-party data importance growing
- Fingerprinting adoption increasing

### Mobile-First Shift
- Mobile traffic dominating
- App-based tracking challenges
- Deep linking importance
- Mobile attribution platforms

### Influencer Integration
- Affiliate + influencer hybrid programs
- Social commerce growth

## 8. Key Takeaways

1. **PPL is growing globally** - Lower risk for advertisers, easier for affiliates
2. **Platform consolidation** - All-in-one tools winning
3. **Technical evolution** - S2S tracking becoming mandatory
4. **Regional differences** - Different networks dominate different GEOs
5. **Fraud is significant** - $84B lost globally in 2023
6. **Payout speed matters** - Weekly or faster preferred
7. **Integration is key** - APIs, webhooks essential
