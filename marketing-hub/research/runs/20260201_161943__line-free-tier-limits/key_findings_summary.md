## Confirmed Findings

### 1. US/English LINE Official Account
- **Free limit: 500 messages/month** - CONFIRMED
- Only Communication Plan available (no paid upgrade options)
- No verified accounts available
- No Premium ID available

### 2. Japan LINE
- **Free limit: 200 messages/month** - CONFIRMED
- Full pricing tiers available:
  - Communication: ¥0 (200 msg)
  - Light: ¥5,000 (5,000 msg)
  - Standard: ¥15,000 (30,000 msg + ¥3/additional)

### 3. Thailand LINE
- **Free limit: 300 messages/month** - CONFIRMED (NOT 500)
- **IMPORTANT:** Reduced from 500 to 300 in August 2024
- Pricing tiers:
  - Free: ฿0 (300 msg)
  - Basic: ฿1,280 (15,000 msg)
  - Pro: ฿1,780 (35,000 msg)

### 4. Taiwan LINE
- **Free limit: 200 messages/month** - CONFIRMED
- Pricing tiers:
  - Light: NT$0 (200 msg)
  - Medium: NT$800 (3,000 msg)
  - High: NT$1,200 (6,000 msg + additional available)

### 5. Indonesia LINE
- **Free limit: 500 messages/month** - CONFIRMED
- Only Communication Plan available (no paid options)

### 6. Other Regions
- **Singapore:** 500 messages/month (no paid plans)
- **EU:** Service NOT AVAILABLE
- **Other:** Generally 500-1000 messages/month

## Critical Policy Information

### Multiple Account Creation
- **Maximum:** 100 accounts per Business ID
- No explicit prohibition on creating multiple accounts
- User data must be managed separately per account
- Cannot share user information between accounts

### Account Verification
- Only available in: Japan, Taiwan, Thailand
- NOT available in: USA, Singapore, Indonesia, EU
- Process takes 2-3 business days
- Requires business documentation

### API Rate Limits
- Push messages: 2,000 requests/second
- Reply messages: NOT counted toward quota
- Auto-responses: NOT counted toward quota
